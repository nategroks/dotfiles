About me:

late nights staring at half-finished drawings of triangles that don't connect. i think ettore sottsass would have liked pixel art. memphis design was always about breaking rules — making serious things feel playful, making playful things feel like they matter. that's what i try to do with my little corner of the internet.


Who I'd like to meet:

anyone who still thinks a squiggle is a valid architectural element. people who arrange their desktop icons by color. creatures of the dusk.
